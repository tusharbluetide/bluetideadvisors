Tushar Sinha

Founder

Tushar has two decades of experience in private equity and M&A investment banking. He has worked with names such as Standard Chartered Private Equity, DE Shaw and Asia Growth Capital (spin off from Credit Suisse PE).

He has represented PE firms on the boards of portfolio companies and has been closely involved in transformation projects at portfolio companies. He has been involved in all aspects of the deal cycle from leading origination efforts to portfolio management, investor relations and communication as well as leading portfolio exit strategy.

A keen data science enthusiast, Tushar believes investment managers can derive much benefit from using data strategy and business intelligence to better monitor and manage their portfolio.
